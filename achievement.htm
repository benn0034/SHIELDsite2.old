Achievement Night


"Give thanks to the LORD, call on His name
Make known His deeds among the peoples;
Make them remember that His name is exalted."
Isaiah 12:4

God blesses us each day with strength to achieve all that we do. We think it is encouraging and appropriate to take time to look back at what God has enabled us to do over the past year. Achievement Night is the occasion when our SHIELD family looks back together at the many ways in which we have been blessed and celebrates all the accomplishments of our school year. Below is a list of some of the ways we do this!

Student Displays
Gather together all those wonderful projects your students have done this year to show everyone. This is a great way to remind teachers that they did do some fun things this year. It's also a great way to get some ideas from others about what to do next year! Most students get exicted to show others what they have created!

Recognition of Achievement
Students who register will receive Academic Acheivement awards for completing the school year. We give out certificates for the Governor's Reading award at this time as well. We would also like to recognize any special awards our students have earned. Just let us know and we'll share the joy!

Kindergarten Graduation With kindergarten graduate parents helping to organize this, we celebrate the completion of kindergarten with the little ones!

Senior Graduation Again, with the help of the graduates and parents, we will dedicate a portion of our program to the deserved recognition of our seniors!

We hope you will join us in recognizing the blessing of God and the hard work of our students. We hope the evening will remind each person of a reason to praise God for His faithfulness. Mark the date on your calendars now! Watch for more details in the spring!

If your want to help or are interested in more information, please contact Valerie Harris.
