Membership for the 2015-2016 School year is open!
  
     
 

Welcome to all you families that have decided to homeschool your children! What an exciting journey you are about to embark on! We are glad that you have decided to join us on this homeschool journey during the 2014-2015 school year. SHIELD is a support group in which you can choose to participate as much or as little as you desire. We offer opportunities for both parents and students to be involved in activities that will support your academic, spiritual, and social lives. Please take advantage of some of these opportunities this year.

SHIELD is not a third option accountability
group. We want you to be clear on the fact that membership in SHIELD does not fulfill the third option requirements for legally home schooling in the state of South Carolina. Please read the SC homeschool law so that you are aware of your options.

If you are ready to join, please use the online membership application. Now you can complete the forms and submit the application online.

If you are new to homeschooling check out the information provided in the links to the right.
