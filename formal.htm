Formal


SHIELD offers a formal night in January of each school year for grades 9-12.

This year we are holding our annual Winter Formal at

Hidden Valley Golf Club
147 Excaliber Court
Gaston, SC

Register by February 19 using the form below. The form must be signed by both the student and parent in order to be confirmed. You will receive an email once we receive your completed forms and payment.

The $25 registration fee is due by February 19th, after which there is a late fee of $10. Late registration closes on February 26th.
