Other Homeschool Opportunities


The following is a list of other opportunities that may be of interest to homeschool families. These activities are not endorsed by SHIELD in any way. The list will be continuously updated with new information.


Right Connection Center
RightConnection Center -5175 Sunset Blvd., Suite G (behind McAlister's deli) Lexington, SC -- offers discounted morning classes for homeshoolers in art, karate and music. Call Kathy Thompson (803) 753-8926 or contact kathy@rightconnection.us for details. Click here for more info...

Riverbanks Zoo Homeschool Mondays
Each month riverbanks Zoo offers a set of classes that will focus on a particular topic and include hands-on activities and live animal encounters. Classes are repeated throughout the month and topics change monthly. Click here for more info...

South Carolina State Museum Homeschool Fridays
The State Museum has Home School Fridays on the third Friday of the month, offering programs for homeschoolers of all ages. Click here for more info...
